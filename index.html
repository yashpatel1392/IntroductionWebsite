About Me

I am Yash Patel, a student at University of Massachusetts Lowell, with a major in Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics. I have been interested in programming ever since high school when I joined First Robotics and wrote my first program in Java for one of the classes.

Being part of First Robotics for 3 years of my high school had boosted my interest towards Computer Science. During summer of 2020, I received an opportunity to work as an intern at the Human Robot Interaction Lab at UMass Lowell, where I gained understanding of robotics research.

I returned back to work as an intern with the UMass Lowell NERVE Center and HRI Lab the following summer and since then I have been working as an Undergraduate Research Assistant where my main duties involve writing software in various languages to perform repetitive tasks with various robots to evaluate their capabilities.

My academic interests include robotics and learning new programming languages. My hobbies include watching movies and travelling with my friends and family.
